Video Generation


A fully automated video generation pipeline integrated with n8n — transforms text or inputs into AI-narrated videos with scenes, subtitles, and background media, eliminating manual video editing efforts.

Tech Used

  • JS

Features

  • Ability to generate multiple video variations in a single run.
  • Automatic background music and subtitles support.
  • No human editing required — end-to-end automated production.
