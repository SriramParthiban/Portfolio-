AI NEWS Scrapper


This project scrapes the data using the firecrawl and gets the most important "NEWS" around the world and if needed it can be customised also to get for a particular surrounding alone.

Tech Used

  • N8N
  • JS
  • Firecrawl

Features

  • Scheduled NEWS flow everyday
  • Easy preference settings
  • Gets you most famous NEWS around the world at your preferred time
