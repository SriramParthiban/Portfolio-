AI Resume Analyzer



The AI Resume Analyzer evaluates resumes and scores candidates based on their skills and experience relative to a specific job role. This automation helps recruiters save significant time and money by filtering and highlighting only the most qualified candidates.

Tech Used

  • n8n Automation
  • JavaScript

Features

  • Fully automated — no manual work required
  • Automatically ranks and filters candidates based on job criteria
  • Helps HR teams save time and improve hiring quality
